## Candidate Set

1. `ridge_sp7776_exact`
   - Purpose: high-confidence marginal improvement.
   - Rationale: private copy of the strongest public Ridge-SP settings, with the same family as our current best but heavier public-winning selector parameters.
   - Risk: runtime and public overfit, but the public source has already demonstrated score viability.

2. `ridge_sp45_proj`
   - Purpose: public/domain projection reproduction.
   - Rationale: applies robust per-well polynomial smoothing to `U = TVT + Z - anchor`, matching the idea that formation-relative depth should evolve smoothly along a lateral with faults/dips as deviations.
   - Risk: projection can oversmooth real abrupt structural changes.

3. `ridge_sp7776_proj`
   - Purpose: combine strongest Ridge-SP profile with structural projection.
   - Rationale: if `ridge_sp7776_exact` is directionally right, projecting it in formation-relative coordinates may reduce high-frequency selector artifacts.
   - Risk: combines two aggressive changes.

4. `ridge_sp7776_projdeg3`
   - Purpose: lower-variance projection alternative.
   - Rationale: degree 3 may keep the structural prior while avoiding the degree-5 tendency to follow noise.
   - Risk: may underfit wells with fault blocks or stronger dip changes.

5. `drift_geosteering_infer`
   - Purpose: genuinely different model family.
   - Rationale: independent pretrained geosteering stack; if Ridge/PF has a shared failure mode, this gives a non-collinear shot.
   - Risk: external public model may be less robust than Ridge-SP on hidden private.
